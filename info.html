What is PRAWN?

From the creators,

"Welcome to The Tank. You have lived here for as long as you can remember. Of course, being a fish, your memory isn't that great… PRAWN is a two-hour live-action roleplaying game played in a swimming pool for ten to twenty players and three 'Tank Masters.'"

In essence, PRAWN plays like a murder mystery game. The fish (or whatever other edible aquatic creature you choose to play) have goals, morals, and a storyline that you get to act out in the game. Will your fish escape the tank? Will they be eaten? Or, will they start a gang war? Who knows?!

Bring your bathing suits and any other pool toys you may have. All the rules will be explained beforehand so don't worry if you've never play a tabletop RPG. The event will be a great way to hang out, have some fun, and meet new friends!

When and Where?

PRAWN will begin on August 31st at 4:00 pm; we will meet in the seating area next to the Tim Horton's in the Athletics Building, then we will move to the pool at 4:30 pm to commence play. The fun will last until 6:30 pm.
